Lumbercorp is a wholesale supplier of timber products to the New Zealand, Australian and Pacific Island markets as well as a provider of custom Kiln drying, CCA and LOSP treatment and timber machining.

Located at Ohinewai in the North Waikato, Lumbercorp is ideally situated to service the Auckland, Waikato and Bay of Plenty markets.

Lumbercorp also operates a state of the art LOSP plant at Te Puke in the Bay of Plenty. This plant’s proximity to local timber manufactures and the Tauranga wharves is ideal for product that requires treating for export as well as wrapping and unitising.
